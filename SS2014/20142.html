Bulletin

The 27th Spring School on Particles and Fields in Taiwan (SS2014) will be hosted by Chang Gung University, from April 2 to April 5, 2012 at the Shengen Lecture Hall, CGU.

The speakers and topics include

  • Masayasu Harada (Nagoya University, Japan): Hadron phenomenology based on holographic QCD models
  • Shih-Chieh Hsu (University of Washington, USA) : LHC Physics
  • Peter Mészáros (Pennsylvania State University, USA) : Gamma Ray Bursts, and connections to Cosmology, Ultra-high Energy Cosmic Rays and Neutrinos
    • *George Fuller (University of California, San Diego, USA) : Neutrino Astrophysics
    * to be confirmed
    Related information and online registration can be accessed at http://memo.cgu.edu.tw/ss2014/ This series of schools has been the major annual meeting in high energy theory for students, postdocs and faculty in Taiwan since 1986. On behalf of the organizing committee, we cordially invite your participation.

    If local students (master and doctor levels) cannot get support from their groups, their transportation and accommodations shared with roommates may be provided by the school after review by the committee. The application for financial support should be submitted as soon as possible to ss2014cgu@gmail.com
