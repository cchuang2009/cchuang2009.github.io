Travel Information

Chang Gung University (CGU) is located at the Linkou-Kweishan area, which is roughly midway between the Taoyuan International Airport and the Taipei Songshan Airport. It you are from Taoyuan International Airport, we recommend that you take a taxi ( ~NT 800, ~45 minutes) to CGU or the nearby hotel, since it requires more transfers and takes more time with the public transportation. If you are from Taipei Songshan Airport, you may take a taxi ( ~ NT 700 , ~40 minutes) or the buses. We provide the following public transportation for your reference. Note that you should always try to get to Chang Gung Memorial Hospital (CGMH) Linkou Branch first, then take a taxi (~NT150) or the free CGMH bus to CGU (~10 minutes).

Public transportation from the airports

  • 1. From Taoyuan International Airport : take Kuo-Kuang bus ,Route 1812, to Taipei Main Station (~55 minutes, NT 125), then take CGMH bus or Formosa Fairway to CGMH Linkou Branch (~45 minutes, NT 35, prepare your own changes ), then take the free CGMH bus or a taxi to CGU
  • 2. From TSA Taipei Songshan Airport: Go to Bus Platform NO.3 (use Easy Card or prepare your own changes). There are three options:(folded)
    • (i) Take Route 945 to CGMH Linkou Branch (first stop off the National Express Way, 40~50 minutes and NT 30), then take a taxi or the free CGMH bus to CGU.
    • (ii) Take Route 5116 to CGMH Linkou Branch (first stop off the National Express Way, 40~50 minutes and NT 41), then take a taxi or the free CGMH bus to CGU.
    • (iii) Take Route 1211 all the way to CGU. It takes a longer time, 100-120 minutes, and costs NT 48.
