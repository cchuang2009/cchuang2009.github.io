Transportation

  • 1. From Taipei Main Station (Taiwan Railway, Taiwan High Speed Rail, Taipei Metro Rapid Transit):
    take CGMH shuttle bus (Formosa Fairway) to CGMH Linkou Branch (40~50 minutes, NT 35, prepare your own changes ), then take a taxi or the free CGMH bus to CGU.
  • 2. From Chang Gung Memorial Hospital , Taipei Branch: take CGMH shuttle bus (Formosa Fairway) to Linkou Branch (40~50 minutes, NT 35, prepare your own changes), then take a taxi or the free CGMH bus to CGU.
  • 3. From Taipei City Hall Bus Station: take Route 1211 bus all the way to CGU. It costs NT 55 (use the Easy Card or prepare your own changes), but is very time-consuming (~ 2 hours).
  • 4. From Banqiao Main Station Taiwan Railway, Taiwan High Speed Rail, Taipei Metro Rapid Transit):
    take Route 920 bus to CGMH Linkou Branch (first stop off the National Express Way, ~50 minutes and NT 30), then take a taxi or the free CGMH bus to CGU.

Driving direction for local participants (In Traditional Chinese)

  • 1.請自行列印 SS2014海報 作為免費臨時停車證。
  • 2.國道一號五楊高架於"林口 龜山"地區並無閘道，請行駛國道一號平面路段。
  • 3.請於國道一號 "林口 龜山" 出口 (exit 41A) 下交流道 → 往龜山方向 → 直行文化一路到底(約4 km) → 直接通過中正體育園區(國立體育大學)正門警衛,無需停車 → 志清湖前 T字路口左轉 → 左邊即可看見長庚大學校門。
  • 4.於門口警衛哨暫停，出示列印之SS2014海報，並按指示牌將車停於指定之停車場。請務必將SS2014海報置於檔風玻璃下以為識別,並按規定停車, 否則校方將可能鎖輪罰款。
