Welcome to SS2014

Started in 1986, the annual Spring School on Particles and Fields has always been the most important gathering for all high energy theorists in Taiwan, including students, postdocs and faculty members.

The 27th Spring School on Particles and Fields in Taiwan will be hosted by Chang Gung University at Taoyuan, Taiwan during April 2-5, 2014. The four topics that will be covered this year are Gamma-Ray Burst and Cosmology, Hadron Phenomenology, LHC Physics, and Neutrino Astrophysics. On each topic, there will be four 60-minute lectures.

The deadline of registration is March 17, 2014. If you want us to arrange your accommodation, please register by February 19, 2014.

We hope that all participants and speakers will enjoy this event, and we thank the generous contributions from our sponsors.
